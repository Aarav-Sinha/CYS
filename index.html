About the Fest

A Forum for Bold Minds

Critically Yours is an intra-school humanities festival organised by the students of the Humanities stream at Delhi Public School Megacity, Kolkata. At its heart lies deep engagement with political science, governance, diplomacy, and global affairs.

From simulated parliaments to geopolitical crises, every event challenges conventional thinking and sharpens analytical discourse.
